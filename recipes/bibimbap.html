Bibimbap

Go back

Description :

Bibimbap (also called Bibimba or Bibimbop) or Dolsot Bibimbap is a typical Korean meal that literally means "mixed rice".

The meal consists of a bowl of steamed white rice topped with vegetables, beef (sometimes tofu or chicken to suit tastes), a whole egg, and kochujang (Korean chili pepper paste).

Ingredients :

  • ¾ to 1 ½ c rice
  • ¾ c kimchi
  • 1 lb steak
  • 2 small carrots
  • 10 oz spinach
  • 8 oz bean sprouts - with beans attached
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cucumber
  • ¼ c + 2 tsp sesame oil
  • ¼ c ground sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp grated garlic
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (peanut, olive, etc)
  • ¼ c soy sauce
  • salt

Steps :

  1. Start the rice cooking in a rice cooker or pot.
  2. While the rice is cooking make the namurus (side dishes).
  3. Then make the beef soboro.
  4. Finally, assemble the ingredients in the bowls and cook them.
  5. Grate the garlic and grind the sesame seeds for all the namurus as once. Sesame seeds expand when ground; 2 or 3 tablespoons of sesame seeds will make ¼ cup of ground sesame seeds.
